Frequently asked

Everything you need to know about Aria AI.

What is Aria AI?

Aria AI is your AI Chief of Staff — a fully autonomous AI personal assistant that lives on WhatsApp. She comes with 15+ built-in skills including web search, browser automation, image and video generation, PDF creation, calendar management, email sending, voice replies, and much more. We configure, train, and deploy her specifically for your business in 3–5 days.

How is Aria different from a normal chatbot?

Most chatbots just answer questions from a pre-set script. Aria can actually execute tasks — she browses websites and takes screenshots, generates professional images and videos, creates PDF proposals and invoices, manages your Google Calendar, sends emails, sets reminders, generates QR codes, and even creates AI avatar spokesperson videos. She's not a chatbot — she's a Chief of Staff.

Do I need to be technical to use Aria?

Not at all. We handle everything — setup, training, configuration, deployment. You just scan one QR code to connect WhatsApp, and you're ready. Ongoing use is just chatting with Aria on WhatsApp like you'd chat with any contact. Say "generate an image of a sunset" or "remind me to call John at 3pm" — it's that simple.

What are the 15+ skills Aria has?

Aria's skills include: Web Search, Browser Automation (screenshots, form filling), AI Image Generation (Flux), AI Video Generation, PDF Generation (proposals, invoices), Calendar Management, Reminders & Scheduled Tasks, Voice Replies (English TTS), Weather (any city), QR Code Generator, URL Shortener, Email Sending, News Briefing, Contact Book, and AI Avatar Videos (talking head spokesperson). The skills available depend on your plan.

Can I customise my assistant's name and personality?

Yes — including the name. Your assistant can be called Aria, Max, Zara, or anything you like. We train her specifically on your business — your services, pricing, policies, tone of voice, and workflow preferences. She gets smarter over time as we refine based on real conversations. To add new info anytime, just tell your assistant directly on WhatsApp.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Enterprise plan clients get their own dedicated VPS — your data never goes to shared servers. All conversations are encrypted. We never sell or share your data. Your knowledge base, contacts, and calendar data belong entirely to you.
