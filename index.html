Frequently asked

Everything you need to know about Aria AI.

What is Aria AI?

Aria AI is a done-for-you AI assistant built on Openclaw — the world's most powerful personal AI platform. We configure, train, and deploy it specifically for your business on WhatsApp. Unlike a simple FAQ bot, Aria can actually execute tasks, browse websites, and complete workflows on your behalf.

How is Aria different from a normal chatbot?

Most chatbots just answer questions from a pre-set script. Aria can actually execute tasks — browse websites, fill forms, make bookings, check stock levels, and complete multi-step workflows autonomously. He also escalates intelligently to you when human judgment is needed, with full context.

Do I need to be technical to use Aria?

Not at all. We handle everything — setup, training, configuration, deployment. You just scan one QR code to connect WhatsApp, and you're ready. Ongoing use is just chatting with Aria on WhatsApp like you'd chat with any contact.

What happens if Aria can't answer something?

Aria escalates to you via Slack (Pro/Enterprise) or WhatsApp, with a full summary of the customer's question so you can respond personally. The customer receives a polite holding message so they never feel ignored.

Can I customise my assistant's name, personality and knowledge?

Yes — including the name. Your assistant can be called Aria, Max, Zara, or anything you like. We train it specifically on your business — your services, pricing, policies, tone of voice, and escalation rules. It gets smarter over time as we refine based on real conversations. To add new info anytime, just tell your assistant directly on WhatsApp.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Aria AI runs on your own private VPS — your data never goes to shared servers. All conversations are end-to-end encrypted. We never sell or share your data. Your knowledge base belongs entirely to you.
